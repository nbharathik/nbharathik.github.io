Bharathi Kannan N.

I am a PhD student at the Marine Data Science Lab, University of Rostock, Germany. My research focuses on Generative AI for Building Information Modeling (BIM), where I work on the Show2Instruct project.

Previously, I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science (Visual Computing) at the University of Siegen, where my thesis explored depth fusion techniques for transformer-based video object detection.

Email / GitHub / LinkedIn / Google Scholar / X (Twitter)

Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of Large Language Models and multimodal AI, with a particular focus on tool-augmented LLMs, multimodal spatial reasoning, and generative methods for BIM/CAD automation.
